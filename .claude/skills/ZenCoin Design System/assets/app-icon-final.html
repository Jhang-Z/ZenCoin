The open ring.

A brushstroke that almost closes. The opening at the upper-right is the breath — what makes this enso, not a circle. It's 记账 by attention, not by counting: the ring leaves room for what's not yet there.

Construction
Ring radius 256 on a 1024 grid (50% of canvas). Stroke 56px — 5.5% of canvas, matches the visual weight of `display` numerals. Opening sits between −15° → 75°, sweeping clockwise backwards (large-arc=1, sweep=0). The opening points up-right because that's where Western readers look first — the ring receives them.
